Train Tracked

A 'better' UK train tracking app. Created as part of a Level 5 Computer Science project at the University of Portsmouth

Developed using Flutter, Dart and the Rail Delivery Group's delightful Darwin API

While currently closed-source, both the front-end and back-end intermediary API will become fully open-source under the GPL-2.0 license, after the grading and moderation period (Late summer 2024). Check here for updates

I intend to release Train Tracked on the Google Play Store SoonTM, and a direct download now (Get it here, updated 15-01-2024). However, an iOS release is looking unlikely as I do not own any Apple devices (Why Apple, do I need a mac to publish for iOS?)
